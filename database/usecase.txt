Customer - The person who wants to hire a packing and moving service.
Admin - The person who manages the website and interacts with the customers.
Mover - The person who actually performs the packing and moving service.
Payment Gateway - The external system that handles online payments.
Some of the use cases that the actors might engage in are:

Customer
Search for packing and moving services.
Request a quote.
Schedule a service.
Track the progress of the service.
Make a payment.
Cancel the service.
Admin
Manage customer accounts.
Manage mover accounts.
Manage service requests.
Manage payments.
Mover
Accept a service request.
Update the status of the service.
Update the location of the service.
Payment Gateway
Process payments.
